ARCSIX 2024 — Instrument & Dataset Reference Draft — work in progress

This page provides a quick-access inventory of all instruments, datasets, and data archives from the ARCSIX 2024 Arctic airborne campaign (NASA P-3B, NASA G-III, SPEC Learjet, and supporting ground/satellite datasets). For full instrument descriptions see Taylor et al. (2026, in prep.), ESSD. For questions about specific datasets, contact the listed PI or the NASA/LaRC data archive team.

How to use: Click a section header to expand a category  ·  Click ▶ Variables on any instrument row to see key measured quantities and uncertainties  ·  ⚠️ yellow cells mark where uncertainty information is still needed  ·  Links shown as [t.b.d.] are pending — if you can fill one in, please use the collaboration spreadsheet (link TBD) or contact Sebastian Schmidt.
